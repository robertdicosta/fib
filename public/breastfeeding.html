Breastfeeding Classes in Peoria, AZ

Your little bundle of joy will be arriving soon, and you want to make sure that you aren't overwhelmed when they get here, we can help make sure you are prepared on feeding them and yourselves. We want you to be able to make informed decisions when it comes to breastfeeding or bottle feeding. We recommend taking our breast-feeding class around 7-8 months of your pregnancy and can be combined with your childbirth classes.

Although it is a natural instinct to breastfeed for both you and your baby, it is still something you want to be prepared for since it is still new for you and your baby. Breastfeeding doesn't always come easy but when you take our breastfeeding class you can be prepared for the benefits of breastfeeding, the dos and don'ts of breastfeeding, diet, pumping and storing breastmilk and more. Being a first-time mom, we want you to feel comfortable feeding your baby and knowing how often to feed.

Our Breast-feeding class allows you and your partner to be prepared and have your questions answered before your baby arrives. Make sure to get scheduled for our breastfeeding class today.
