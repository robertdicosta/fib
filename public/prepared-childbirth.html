Childbirth Classes in Peoria, AZ

Is it time to get ready for childbirth? We recommend you start your childbirth classes during your 7-8 month, that way you can be prepared even if your baby happens to arrive early. We understand that things don't always go as planned and each person is unique, so we want you to be ready. Our childbirth classes are taught by an RN, Certified Childbirth Educator in a peaceful environment or even in your own private setting. We know that each family needs to have their own personalized technique through childbirth, and we are here to offer that.

We want you and your coach to be as prepared and comfortable as you can when it comes to childbirth. We will help you learn and be prepared for procedures, tests, labor, pain management, recovery after birth, techniques to cope with labor, eliminate your fears, and so much more. Our childbirth classes are a good way to get prepped for your labor and birth, with a bonus that you can meet other expecting parents and make connections for kids that will be around the same age and helping each other through this new adventure. Our classes go into more detail than what you will get from the hospital as well as we offer alternative pain and birthing techniques, so you have another alternative other than traditional medications.

At Families in bloom we understand how much joy it is to become a parent and we want you to be prepared now before your baby arrives allowing you an easier transition. Make sure to give us a call today to get scheduled for of our child birthing classes.
